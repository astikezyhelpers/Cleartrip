Customer Segment Analysis

Market Segmentation

Segment Characteristics

Premium Segment (15%)

  • Affluent HNI customers from urban centers
  • High-touch service expectations
  • Personalized travel experiences
  • In-person client meetings preferred

Mid-Market Segment (62%)

  • Urban middle-class travelers
  • Mix of online/offline booking behaviors
  • Value convenience and quality
  • Price-conscious but quality-focused

Value Segment (23%)

  • Tier II & III city customers
  • Primarily offline bookings
  • Local agent relationships
  • Price-sensitive travel decisions

Strategic Focus

Mid-market segment represents our primary opportunity - 62% of market with growing digital adoption and quality expectations align perfectly with Cleartrip's value proposition.
