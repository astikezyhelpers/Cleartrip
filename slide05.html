Emerging Travel Preferences

  • Experiential Travel: Authentic, immersive experiences over standard sightseeing. 68% of Indian travelers use social media for inspiration.
  • Generational Shifts: Gen Z & Millennials driving luxury travel growth, prioritizing experiences over material possessions.
  • Tech-Enabled Journeys: 70.5% of travel site traffic from mobile devices. AI-driven personalization increasing conversions by 10-15%.
  • Specialized Travel: Solo travel, women-only packages, wellness tourism. Multi-generational family travel gaining popularity.
